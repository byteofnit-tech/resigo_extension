## üõ† Installation Guide
Since Resigo is an **unpacked extension**, follow these steps to install:

1. **Download Extension:** Download then extract the zip.
2. **Enable Developer Mode:** Go to `chrome://extensions` and toggle **Developer mode**.
3. **Load Extension:** Click **Load unpacked** and select the `extension` folder.
4. **Pin for Access:** Click the puzzle icon and **Pin** Resigo to your toolbar.

## üìñ Usage Guide

Follow these steps to get the most out of **Resigo**.

---

### üìÑ PDF to JPG
*Convert entire PDF documents into separate high-quality image files.*

1. **Select** `PDF ‚Üí JPG` from the dropdown menu (default).
2. **Drag & Drop** your PDF file into the designated drop zone.
3. **Adjust** the **Scale** (resolution) and **Quality** sliders in the settings panel.
4. **Preview** the converted pages in the gallery that appears below.
5. **Download** by clicking **Save** on individual pages or dragging them to your desktop.

---

### üñºÔ∏è JPG to PDF
*Combine multiple images into a single, professional PDF document.*

1. **Select** `JPG ‚Üí PDF` from the dropdown menu.
2. **Drag & Drop** one or more image files simultaneously.
3. **Process:** The extension will automatically merge the files.
4. **Output:** A `Converted.pdf` file will appear in your gallery.
5. **Save** to download your new consolidated PDF.

---

### üìâ PDF Compression
*Reduce file size significantly while maintaining readability.*

1. **Select** `Compress PDF` from the dropdown menu.
2. **Drop** your large PDF file into the tool.
3. **Analyze:** A staging area will show you the original file size.
4. **Tune:** Use the **Scale** and **Quality** sliders to find your perfect compression ratio.
5. **Action:** Click **Compress Now** to begin the local processing.
6. **Download** the optimized PDF from the gallery.

---

### üìè Image Resizing
*Resize images to specific dimensions with precision control.*

1. **Select** `Resize Image` from the dropdown menu.
2. **Drop** your image file into the interface.
3. **Dimensions:** Enter your desired **Width** or **Height**.
* *Note: The aspect ratio is locked by default (click the lock icon to unlock).*
4. **Review:** Check the estimated output size before processing.
5. **Process & Save:** Click the process button and download your resized image.
